Support & Help

Get instant answers to your questions

Everything you need to know about Dassh's AI-powered hiring platform. Browse common questions by category or reach out to our support team for personalized assistance.

How quickly can I get started with Dassh?

Setup is quick and simple. After requesting a demo, our team will configure your workflows and integrations. Most clients are up and running within days, not weeks.

What do I need to provide during setup?

Just your job descriptions and hiring preferences. Our team handles all technical configuration, workflow setup, and integration with your existing systems.

Is there a minimum commitment period?

We offer flexible plans to match your hiring needs. Whether you need Dassh for a single role or ongoing recruitment, we have options that work for you.

How does Dassh's AI screening work?

Dassh uses advanced AI to analyze resumes beyond keywords, evaluating candidates based on role relevance, education, experience, skills, and job requirements for intelligent ranking.

How does the AI calling feature work?

Our AI conducts initial screening calls to gather key information like salary expectations, notice period, location preferences, and experience details, saving your team valuable time.

Is the AI biased in any way?

Our AI is designed to reduce bias by focusing on job-relevant qualifications rather than demographic factors. We continuously monitor and improve our algorithms for fairness.

Can I customize screening criteria for different roles?

Absolutely! Dassh allows you to create custom workflows tailored for each role or client. You can define specific traits, scoring criteria, and requirements.

What integrations does Dassh support?

Dassh integrates seamlessly with popular CRM and ATS systems. We support major platforms and can set up custom integrations based on your specific needs.

Can I adjust the AI's scoring preferences?

Yes, you can fine-tune scoring weights for different criteria like experience, education, skills, and cultural fit to match your specific hiring priorities.

Is Dassh suitable for small companies?

Yes! Dassh scales with your needs. Whether you're hiring occasionally or processing thousands of applications, our platform adapts to your volume and requirements.

What about HR agencies and recruitment firms?

Perfect for agencies! Manage multiple clients with different requirements, customize workflows per client, and handle high-volume screening with consistent quality.

Do you offer enterprise features?

Yes, we provide enterprise-grade security, custom integrations, dedicated support, and advanced analytics for large organizations with complex hiring needs.
